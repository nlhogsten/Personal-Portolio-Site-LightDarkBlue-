Custom Picture Framing in Hawaii

Framing art is important for artists preparing to display their work and for the patrons of art looking to display works in their homes and businesses. Our frames are all hand made in Honolulu, Hawai‘i. Materials are sourced from local hardware providers, and every wood cut, stain, and assembly is done here on Oahu.

Floating Frames in Hawaii

Our specialty is in constructing floating frames - this type of frame is meant to give art the illusion of depth and a 3D floating effect inside of the picture frame. This style is inspired by the 20th century framing technique known as stripping. In recent years floating frames have become increasingly popular amongst artists and interior decorators.
