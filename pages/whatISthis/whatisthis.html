Light Dark Blue is the online basepoint for the Honolulu based arts collective founded by Nate Hogsten.

Social media has long been the dominant method for creatives to present their work, gather a following, and grow as an artist. But the lack of customization in the way work is presented and scraping by at the mercy of profit driven algorithms designed by tech companies will always be a barrier to the artistic process.

We currently offer services in carpentry and woodworking, murals and abstract art, graphic design, and web development.
