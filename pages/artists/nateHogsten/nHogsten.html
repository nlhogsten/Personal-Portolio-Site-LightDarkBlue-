Nate Hogsten

This Honolulu based artist's interests center on abstract painting, sculpture, and graphic design. Since graduating from the University of Hawai‘i at Mānoa, art wasn't on the radar due to traditional career advisories. While working full time he founded the Kaimuki Compost Collective to divert emission producing waste from Oahu's landfills and unlocked time to pursue burgeoning artistic ambitions.
